Welcome to The Church of Pentecost - New York District

Our church is dedicated to spreading the gospel of Christ, serving the community, and nurturing spiritual growth. We are a vibrant congregation united in faith, prayer, and service to God and humanity.

Our Mission

To lead people to Christ and equip them to become devoted disciples who transform society through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

We envision a Christ-centered church impacting our community with love, integrity, and hope, building a legacy of faith for future generations.

Core Values

  • Faith in God
  • Love and Compassion
  • Integrity and Service
  • Unity and Fellowship
  • Community Outreach
